# StackPay

StackPay is a revolutionary stablecoin payment platform built on the Stacks blockchain. It leverages the security and stability of Bitcoin through the Stacks layer to ensure safe transactions. By using USDCx, users can enjoy the benefits of a stable digital currency for their daily needs.

## Features

### Send & Receive USDCx
Our platform allows you to seamlessly send and receive USDCx across the Stacks network with minimal fees. Experience near-instant settlement times for all your transfers, ensuring your money is where it needs to be when you need it.
### Utility Bill Payments
Pay for essential services like airtime and data directly from your wallet. Settle electricity bills and cable TV subscriptions using your stablecoin balance. Enjoy the convenience of handling all your utility payments in one secure place.

## Technology

### Blockchain
StackPay is built on the robust Stacks blockchain, inheriting Bitcoin's security. This ensures that every transaction is settled with the finality of the world's most secure network.
### Currency
We utilize USDCx, a stablecoin on Stacks, to provide a reliable medium of exchange. This allows users to avoid the volatility typically associated with cryptocurrencies.
## Getting Started
To get started with StackPay, you will need a Stacks wallet. We recommend using Xverse or Hiro Wallet for the best experience.
